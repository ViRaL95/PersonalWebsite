About Varun

Varun Rao is a Student, a Full Stack Developer, and a Musician


Varun hopes to one day become a Software Engineer who specializes in the medical industry.

Why The Medical Industry?

Varun has logged over 200 hours volunteered at New York Hospital Queens. He has interned for Coeo' Labs, a startup that is producing a medical device to reduce ventilator associated pnuemonia, and is currently doing research at Stony Brook University to help create a device that can measure muscle movement in patients who have recently undergone surgery.

Current Skills:

He is currently skilled in C, C++, Java, HTML, and PHP and is able to deliver mobile-responsive web applications. His upcoming goals are to build his web development skills by deploying more projects involving WordPress, PHP, SQL, and jQuery. His future goals are to build an app for either IOS or Android.

Up Close and Personal

Varun currently attends Stony Brook University where he is majoring in Computer Engineering and Applied Mathematics and Statistics. In his spare time, he enjoys learning new programming skills. He also loves playing music, hiking different mountain ranges and traveling. Varun has undergone 8 years of training to play the Tabla, a Hindustani classical drum, and has performed in various venues across Long Island and New York City. He has traveled to India, Dubai, Bahamas, Italy, Mexico, and Puerto Rico and looks to continue to travel across the world.
